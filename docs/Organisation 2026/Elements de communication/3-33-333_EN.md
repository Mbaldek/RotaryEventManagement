# Communication pack — 3 / 33 / 333 format

> Message pyramid: **3 words** (hook) → **~33 words** (summary) → **~333 words** (full text).
> Two documents: **A.** competition presentation · **B.** message to incubators (to forward to their startups).
> Reference facts: Key messages kit, EN_full / EN_short, Email to startups EN.

---

## A. Competition presentation

### 3 words
**Pitch. Win. Shine.**

### 33 words
The Rotary Startup Award 2026, a Paris–Berlin pitch competition, spotlights innovative and committed startups. €5,000 for the winner, €1,500 greentech prize. Applications open until March 31 at rotary-startup.org.

### 333 words
**Rotary Startup Award 2026 — the Paris–Berlin pitch competition for innovative and committed startups.**

The Rotary Club of Paris, together with the Rotary Club of Berlin, is launching the 2026 edition of the Rotary Startup Award: a pitch competition open to young companies looking to gain visibility, test their project before a demanding jury, and join a committed international network.

**Why apply**
- **Visibility** before decision-makers, executives and investors.
- A **clear and demanding selection process**, overseen by an independent jury of Rotary members and qualified experts.
- A **€5,000 award** for the main winner, plus a **special environment / greentech prize of €1,500**.
- **Access to an international Paris–Berlin network** and the Rotary community.

**Eligibility (indicative criteria, non-strictly exclusive)**
Startups founded after 01/01/2020, with annual revenue below €500k, having raised less than €800k, French or German, all sectors.

**Timeline**
- Applications: February 1 – March 31 (possible extension to April 15)
- Application screening: April 30
- Pitch sessions: remote
- Grand Final: [date — to be confirmed], in person in [venue — to be confirmed]
- Award ceremony: [date — to be confirmed], at [venue — to be confirmed]

**Apply**
All information and the application form are at **https://rotary-startup.org**.

This is neither a paid scheme nor a commercial partnership: it is a non-profit initiative led by Rotarians committed to entrepreneurship.

## B. Message to incubators (to forward to their startups)

### 3 words
**Reveal your gems.**

### 33 words
Do you support innovative startups? Forward them the Rotary Startup Award 2026, a Paris–Berlin pitch competition: €5,000 + €1,500 greentech, visibility and an international network. Free, no commitment. rotary-startup.org

### 333 words
**Incubators, accelerators: what if the next startup in the spotlight were one of yours?**

Dear Madam, Dear Sir,

The Rotary Club of Paris, together with the Rotary Club of Berlin, is launching the 2026 edition of the Rotary Startup Award, a pitch competition dedicated to innovative and committed startups.

**The quality of the competition rests largely on the quality of sourcing.** That is why we wish to involve a limited number of recognized institutions from the entrepreneurial ecosystem, whose role is central: to identify and showcase the most promising projects you support today, or have supported in the past. Your institution is a pivotal player: it directly contributes to the quality, credibility and impact of the competition.

👉 **This is neither a commercial partnership nor a paid scheme.** Your involvement is voluntary, with no operational constraints and no obligation to sit on the jury. Should you wish to contribute more actively, we would be glad and honored to welcome you to our juries and the Rotary community.

**What your startups gain**
- Visibility before decision-makers, executives and investors.
- A clear and demanding selection process, with an independent jury (Rotary members + qualified experts).
- €5,000 for the winner + €1,500 special environment / greentech prize.
- Access to a committed international Paris–Berlin network.

**Eligibility (indicative)**
Founded after 01/01/2020, revenue < €500k, raised < €800k, French or German, all sectors.

**How to forward**
Simply pass the call for applications on to your startups (a ready-to-use kit — email, newsletter, social media — is available on request). All information and the form are at **https://rotary-startup.org**.

We would be delighted to count your institution among the key players of this edition, and remain available for a simple conversation.
